3. How We Use Your Information

  • Display real-time personnel locations to authorized administrators only
  • Generate attendance and performance reports
  • Coordinate emergency responses to SOS alerts
  • Maintain security incident records
  • Verify patrol routes and checkpoint completion
  • Send push notifications for shifts, tasks, and alerts

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script, operated on [YOUR ORGANIZATION'S] Google Workspace account. Data is transmitted exclusively over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties outside the organization.

5. Location Data

The App collects GPS location data while you are on duty and have the app open or running in the background. Background location is used only during active patrol shifts to maintain continuous tracking for safety purposes. Location data is visible only to authorized administrators within your organization.

You may stop location tracking at any time by stopping the patrol in the app or revoking location permission in your device settings. Note that location tracking is required for shift compliance.

6. Photos and Media

Photos captured through the App (for incident reports) are stored only within the organization's Google Workspace and are not shared externally. Camera permission is requested only when you choose to attach a photo.

7. Data Retention

Location history is retained for 90 days. Attendance and incident records are retained for a minimum of 1 year for compliance purposes. Account data is retained for the duration of employment. You may request data deletion by contacting your HR or system administrator.

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for background location tracking

9. Security

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS only
  • Session token-based authentication
  • Role-based access control (admin vs. personnel)

The App is intended for adults (18+) employed as security personnel. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18 years of age.
